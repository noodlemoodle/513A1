Applications of Wavelet Transform


Generally, an approximation to discrete wavelet transform is used for data compression if a signal is already sampled, and the continuous wavelet transform for signal analysis.
Thus, the discrete wavelet transform approximation is commonly used in engineering and computer science, while the continuous wavelet transform in scientific research.

Wavelets as a Representation of a Signal


Wavelets are useful for describing non-continuous signals with abrupt discontinuity, due to their time-localized behavior.
